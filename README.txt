Take payments on your store using UnDosTres.

== Description ==

Accept multiple types of payment and more directly on your store with the UnDosTres payment gateway for WooCommerce.

= Take payments easily and directly on your store =

The UnDosTres plugin extends WooCommerce allowing you to take payments directly on your store via UnDosTres API.

UnDosTres is available for Store Owners and Merchants in:

* Mexico
